About Us

The Junior Economic Club is dedicated to fostering an understanding of economics and finance among high school students. We believe that a strong foundation in these fields is essential for future success, and our club provides a platform for students to learn, collaborate, and compete.

Our activities include weekly discussions, guest speaker sessions from industry professionals, and participation in state and national economic competitions. We welcome all students, regardless of their prior knowledge or experience.
