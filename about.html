Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Junior Economic Club?

The Junior Economic Club is a student organization that aims to foster an interest in economics and business among young individuals through various activities and events.

Who can join the Junior Economic Club?

The club is open to all students who have an interest in economics, business, and related fields. There are no specific prerequisites for joining.

What kind of events does the club organise?

The club organises a variety of events including guest lectures, workshops, networking sessions, and competitions related to economics and business.

How often does the club meet?

The club typically meets once a week during the academic year. Additional meetings may be scheduled for special events or projects.

Is there a membership fee?

Membership is usually free, but there may be occasional fees for specific events or activities. Any fees will be communicated in advance.
